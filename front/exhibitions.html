Starry Visions: The World of Van Gogh

May 11, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Vincent Van Gogh

This exhibition delves into the luminous and expressive world of Vincent van Gogh, showcasing some of his most celebrated works, including The Starry Night, Sunflowers, and Wheatfield with Crows. Visitors will explore his artistic evolution, personal struggles, and lasting influence on modern art. The exhibition opens on May 11, 2025 at Kay's Originals, with a special opening night featuring guest speakers and a guided tour.

Silent Light: The Art of Edward Hopper

June 8, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Edward Hoppper

Featuring Edward Hopper’s most evocative paintings, Silent Light explores his mastery of light and shadow, depicting moments of quiet introspection and loneliness in everyday American life. The exhibition opens on June 8, 2025 at Kay's Originals, with an exclusive opening reception and a discussion on Hopper’s artistic legacy.

Dreamscapes: The Surreal World of Salvador Dalí

July 26, 2025

Exhibition Info

Artist: Salvador Dalí

Step into the dreamlike universe of Salvador Dalí, where melting clocks, elongated figures, and bizarre landscapes challenge perceptions of reality. Through paintings, sculptures, and multimedia installations, Dreamscapes offers an immersive journey into Dalí’s surreal visions. The exhibition opens on July 26, 2025 at Kay's Originals, featuring a surrealist-themed opening event with live performances and interactive installations.

111 GALLERY AVE, ART CITY, NY 11548 | MON-SAT 10AM - 6PM
